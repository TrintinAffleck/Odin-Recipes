Skillet-Chicken and Rice Recipe

Description

Skillet Chicken is a simple and healthy meal that requires only one pot! So you don't need to do very many dishes afterwards and tastes great!

Recipe

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 4 carrots (medium) diced
  • 2 parsnips diced
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1/3 cup (65g) wild rice rinsed
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown rice rinsed
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 cups (750ml) chicken stock
  • 400g chicken tenderloins-skinless
  • 150g green beanstrimmed & cut in thirds
  • 1/4 bunch of fresh flat leaf parsnips (5oz bunch) roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat and saute carrots and parsnip for 5-10 minutes, or until tender. Season well with salt and pepper.
  2. Add rice and stir through for a minute or two.
  3. Add onion powder, garlic powder and stock. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes, stirring regularly. Adjust seasoning.
  4. Add chicken in an even layer, cover and simmer for another 15 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. (You may need to add a little more stock or water if necessary.)
  5. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside. Add beans and continue to cook for another 4-5 minutes or until beans are just cooked.
  6. Whilst beans cook, dice or shred chicken and return to the skillet.
  7. Divide between bowls, top with parsley and serve.
